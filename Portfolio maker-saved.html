Say Hello

LEO Satellite Comms Subsystem

This project involved the end-to-end design of a high-throughput communication board for a LEO satellite. Key responsibilities included architecting the digital processing core, implementing a high-speed DDR4 memory interface, and ensuring signal integrity for 28 Gbps SERDES channels.

The final design passed rigorous thermal and vibration testing, achieving a 50% increase in data throughput compared to the previous generation while maintaining the same power envelope.

Avionics Power Management

Designed a redundant, fault-tolerant Power Management Unit (PMU) for critical avionics systems. The design featured hot-swappable modules, precise current monitoring, and a CAN bus interface for system health reporting.

Successfully achieved DO-254 compliance for complex electronic hardware in airborne systems. The modular design reduced maintenance time by 75%.

Deep Space RF Transceiver

Developed a custom X-band RF front-end for a deep space probe, focusing on ultra-low noise figure and high linearity under extreme temperature variations. The project required meticulous component selection, custom filter design, and impedance matching for maximum power transfer.

The resulting transceiver demonstrated a receiver sensitivity exceeding requirements by 3 dB, significantly extending the probe's communication range.
