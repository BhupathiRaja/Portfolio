EEE GRADUATE // POWER ELECTRONICS & AEROSPACE

I am an EEE graduate with strong interests in power electronics, battery systems, and advanced energy conversion for EV and aerospace applications.

My work focuses on the simulation and control of complex systems like DC fast chargers, multilevel inverters, and Solid-State Transformers (SSTs) using industry-standard tools like MATLAB/Simulink and LTspice.

1. Design of DC-DC Converter/Regulator

Designed a high-efficiency buck-boost converter for regulated power delivery. Analyzed switching losses and implemented feedback control loops to maintain stability under varying load conditions.

2. Power & Energy Budget Analysis

Conducted comprehensive energy audits for satellite subsystems. Calculated peak vs. average power consumption to dimension solar arrays and battery banks for specific orbital periods.

3. Radiation Effects & Solar-Cell Performance

Simulated degradation of PV cells under proton and electron flux typical of LEO environments. Made a detailed report of POWER calculations with and without cover-glass shielding thickness. Documented comprehensive BOL/EOL values at different temperatures.

4. SST Based DC Fast Charger

Modeled a Solid-State Transformer architecture for EV charging stations. Replaced bulky line-frequency transformers with high-frequency isolated converters to improve power density.

5. Designed EMI Filter

Designed input/output EMI filters to suppress conducted interference in switching power supplies, ensuring compliance with EMC standards (MIL-STD-461G).

6. FMEA, Reliability & Derating Analysis

Performed Failure Mode and Effects Analysis (FMEA) on power distribution units. Applied component derating factors to enhance system longevity and reliability in harsh environments.
